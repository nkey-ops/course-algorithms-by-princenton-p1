Prove that the game cannot end in a tie.
Hint: consider the set of cells reachable from the left side of the board. 

To make a tie by separating opponent's sides is by definition is a win and cannot be a tie.

Because to do that the player needs to 
-> create a complete parallel to opponents side connection of cells.
-> which means perpendicular to player's side.
-> which means a connection between player's sides.
-> which constitutes player's win.
